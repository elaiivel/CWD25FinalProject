OUR LEGACY

Masarej Flower Shop

"When words aren't enough, say it with flowers."



BACK TO HOME



Masarej Flower Shop caters to the flower needs of Bataan, offering both express flower delivery services and convenient walk-in orders. Their extensive selection features a range of farm-fresh blooms, including roses, tulips, gerberas, carnations, and a variety of unique flower bouquets and gift items.



Founded in February 2012, Masarej Flower Shop proudly stands as the pioneer flower shop in Limay, Bataan. The shop is under the ownership of Jhun Gabon, an accomplished International Floral Designer and Competitor, adding a touch of artistry to their offerings.


With their commitment to affordability, they make it easy for you to find the perfect flowers for special occasions like Valentine's Day, anniversaries, birthdays, Mother's Day, sympathy and funeral services, and the festive spirit of Christmas.


The Founder's Blossoming Journey: A Floral Artisan's Tale

Jhun Gabon's remarkable journey began with his studies in floristry and interior design at the Philippine Women's University in Manila, laying the foundation for his blooming career. He honed his skills as a florist in various flower shops in Manila, but his passion for floral artistry led him to the prestigious Burj Al Arab, a five-star hotel often dubbed the world's only 'seven-star' hotel. After gaining international experience, Jhun extended his expertise to a flower shop in Guam, further enriching his craft. Eventually, driven by his passion and expertise, he decided to cultivate his own dream, opening a charming flower shop in Limay, Bataan, where he continues to create exquisite floral arrangements and bring beauty to people's lives.


Masarej Flower Shop: Where International Experience Meets Local Artistry

Journeying from the prestigious Burj Al Arab to the tropical charm of Guam, our founder's passion for floristry found its home in the heart of Bataan, giving birth to Masarej Flower Shop. This blossoming haven marks the culmination of a remarkable odyssey, blending unique international inspirations with finely honed skills and unwavering dedication. More than just a florist, it's an artistic sanctuary offering an unforgettable floral experience. Step into the world of blooms through the eyes of our founder, a true floral artist, the next time you're in Bataan.

Say It With Flowers.

Its enchanting floral arrangements possess the power to articulate profound sentiments and emotions, creating an enchanting and heartfelt language of their own.
